New Android was born

Welcome to social launcher Xoia — your place for learning how to use exceptional Android Web apps.

Create something useful for you

We focused design principles of Xoia-beta around three overarching goals, which are
liked by users and helped developers to create perfect social Android Web apps.

Smart Search

Xoia offers you to use your voice for sending messages, setting alarms, calling friends, and other — solve problems just by speaking with phone. It also interacts with other installed apps, like reserving an airplane flight or buying a ticket to cinema.

Safe Battery

We developed the new power-efficient system, which allows you to safe up to 30% of battery life. It gives you additional time to surfing the web or watching movies during the day. Particularly, if you enable Power-safe mode you’ll continue to receive new email and messages from your friends.

Common Design

Google Now brings you just the right information at just the right time. Weather, traffic, transit schedules, even the latest scores come automatically, just as you need them.

Xoia works on all devices

Step-by-step we are going to integrate HTML5 in Xoia and open for all web-app developers a new SDK,
which allows Web applications to use features of your phone.

Featuring apps

Your apps will gain a new way to inform users about special events and sales.
Also, we introduce a full-personalisation model of targeting and recommendation.
